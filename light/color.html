Color

Color is signed by taking your fingers to your chin and wiggling them in place.
















How to use

Color can be used to prompt your baby to give you the color of something. Just point at the object, and make the sign for color. This is a great intermediate game to play with your baby to help them to learn to develop their understanding of the colors. Of course, before you do this, make sure your baby has at least a few of the basic color signs.
